    The PATRONA puppy website is a website which is actually encyclopedia about all types of dogs. The Famous Veteran Patrona is fond of animals specially Dogs, Cats, Rabbits. With her achievements in the field of serving as veteran, she also had interest in writing books on animals and their behavior. All those years she had came across various type of breeds and their characteristics and behavior. Due to passion of photography she has huge collection of animals pictures of all types discovered. Ms.Patrona wishes to share this images with all for which she decided to develop an website of dogs.
